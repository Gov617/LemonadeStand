Bring Lemonade Class to your classroom

Lemonade Class is a classroom-ready micro-business simulation that helps students practice profit, pricing, costs, and data-driven decision making in a way that feels fun.

Grades 6–7 Chromebook-friendly No accounts required 45–60 min lesson or mini-unit
What pilot teachers receive
  • Printable 1-page teacher guide + lesson flow
  • Quick-start link for students
  • Optional reflection sheet / assessment rubric
  • Direct line to request features + improvements
What we ask from pilot teachers
  • Try it with students at least once
  • Share 3–5 minutes of feedback (Google Form)
  • Optional: send student reflections or class observations
