What is Lemonade Class?

Lemonade Class is a classroom-ready micro-business simulation designed for Grades 6–7. Students run a lemonade stand across multiple “days,” making real decisions about pricing, production, and marketing.

  • Revenue vs. Profit
  • Fixed vs. Variable Costs
  • Pricing & Demand
  • Inventory & Waste
  • Data-Driven Decision Making
