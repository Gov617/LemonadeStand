Lemonade Class

A classroom-ready micro-business simulation for Grades 6–7

What is Lemonade Class?

Lemonade Class is a fast, engaging business simulation where students run a lemonade stand for multiple “days.” Students make three decisions each round:

  • How many cups to produce
  • What price to charge
  • Whether to spend on marketing

The simulation calculates revenue, costs, profit, waste, demand changes, and reputation automatically.

What Students Learn

  • Revenue vs. Profit
  • Fixed vs. Variable Costs (COGS)
  • Demand & Price Sensitivity
  • Inventory & Waste
  • Decision-making using data
  • Business strategy and iteration

How It Fits in Class

  • ✔ One 45–60 minute lesson
  • ✔ 5-day mini unit
  • ✔ 2-week project expansion
  • ✔ Math, Financial Literacy, Social Studies, or Advisory

Frequently Asked Questions

Is this aligned to standards?

Yes. It supports financial literacy, decimal/percentage math, multi-step problem solving, and written justification using data.

Do students need accounts?

No accounts required. The game runs in the browser and resets easily for the next class.

What devices work?

Chromebooks, laptops, tablets — any modern browser.

How long does it take?

Each simulation “day” runs quickly. Most classes complete a full 7-day simulation in one period.

Is there a teacher view?

Yes. Teacher Mode allows settings control and reset options for classroom management.

Classroom Benefits

  • Students immediately see how decisions impact profit
  • Engaging and repeatable
  • Encourages strategic thinking
  • Printable reflection sheet for assessment
